General Rules for the Creative event

  • Software requirement: any text editor.
  • Size of the team: Maximum 3 people.
  • Category: Open
  • Assessment will be done on the basis of:
    • Visual appeal
    • Usability
    • Design principles kept in mind
  • HTML, CSS and JavaScript are allowed. jQuery is allowed and recommended.
  • CSS libraries such as Bootstrap, MDL and W3.CSS are strictly prohibited.
  • jQuery plugins and JavaScript plugins are allowed (except W3.JS).

Preliminary Round

  • Create a website for a ___________.
  • Make it in maximum 3 pages.
  • Submit your entries at xyz@gmail.com

Onsite Round

  • Software requirement: any text editor, Adobe Animate CC.
  • The list of qualified teams shall be posted on our Facebook page and our website (http://byteclub.html-5.me).
  • Students have to bring their own laptops with the required softwares installed.
  • The onsite rules shall be declared on the day of the competition. Be prepared for anything.
